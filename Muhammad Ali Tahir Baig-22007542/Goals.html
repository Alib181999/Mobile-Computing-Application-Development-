Clean X

Goal

This app will aim to provide the service of at home cleaning and disinfecting with supreme class non toxic products and professional staff. The app will especially be useful for people during pandemics such as the corona virus as well as for detailed regular cleaning and disinfecting of households. The app will have a contact us page, where the users will be able to book online appointments for a home cleaning visit of our team, as well as will have a page labelled “our products” , that will have all the products that our team uses on regular basis along with their specifications.
